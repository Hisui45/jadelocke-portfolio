About

As I mentioned before, my name is Jade and I'm a web developer.

Well, web developer primarily, as of right now. I enjoy developing in many languages and creating different types of apps and products. I've been programming for 4 years now. And in those years, problem-solving has always been a great motivator to learn for me. Encounter a problem, learn how to solve it. I was on spring break in high-school. Early morning, I could smell the dew on the grass. Excited to play Minecraft all day, I turn on my desktop and I'm met with a blue screen.


What do you mean the OS system couldn't be found? It's not like it had legs to get up and walk away. Of course, at this point I didn't truly understand what the error meant. Turning it on and off again didn't work, neither did any of the pre-installed recovery options. After hours of searching and trying different suggested fixes, none of which worked. I found that I could reinstall Windows through a USB drive, but doing so would completely wipe the computer.



That computer had everything. Pictures, videos, projects, and most importantly my Minecraft world save files. It goes without saying that I was not inclined to go that route. I was determined to have my cake and eat it too. So, I kept searching for an alternative solution. 3 days had passed since the blue screen made its appearance.


What if I could just get the files off the computer?

Eureka! The hope and joy that had been slowly draining out of my body was replenishing itself. Several more hours of searching and trying numerous things and then I found it. A program that goes on a USB drive that can access my files without the OS booting. I grabbed every single external drive I could get my hands on. Transferring my important files, I've never felt more smug in my life. The battle was done. And I had won.


That was 6 years ago, but I still bring the same motivation, passion, (and sometimes desperation) to every challenge I face, whether it's chasing down an OS system with legs or developing websites and applications.



Check out my projects here. If you haven't yet and see my dedication for yourself.
